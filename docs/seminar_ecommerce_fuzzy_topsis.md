## Slide 9. Step 2: Construct fuzzy decision matrix
Noi dung tren slide:

## Slide 11. Step 4: Weighted matrix, FPIS va FNIS
Noi dung tren slide:
- Tu ma tran chuan hoa, tac gia lap Weightage Matrix o Table 7
- Sau do xac dinh hai moc tham chieu:
  - FPIS: Fuzzy Positive Ideal Solution
  - FNIS: Fuzzy Negative Ideal Solution
- Hieu don gian:
  - FPIS la diem ly tuong tot nhat
  - FNIS la diem ly tuong xau nhat
- Bai bao su dung cac moc ly tuong fuzzy de lam chuan khi tinh khoang cach

Ghi chu trinh bay:
Day la buoc cot loi cua TOPSIS. Tu day tro di, van de khong con la “ai duoc danh gia tot” nua, ma la “ai gan diem ly tuong tot nhat va xa diem xau nhat nhat”.

## Slide 12. Step 5: Closeness from FPIS and FNIS
Noi dung tren slide:
- Tac gia tinh khoang cach cua tung loi ich toi FPIS va FNIS
- Table 8 ghi lai 2 gia tri cho moi loi ich:
  - di+: khoang cach den FPIS
  - di-: khoang cach den FNIS
- Cong thuc closeness coefficient:
  - CCi = di- / (di+ + di-)
- Gia tri CC cang gan 1 thi phuong an cang tot
- Vi du B1:
  - di+ = 1.95
  - di- = 3.21
  - nen CC xap xi 0.620

Ghi chu trinh bay:
Can noi ro o day: B1 dung dau khong phai vi cam tinh, ma vi no vua xa nghiem xau nhat, vua tuong doi gan nghiem tot nhat, nen closeness coefficient cao nhat.

## Nhan xet nhanh de tra loi neu bi hoi
- Vi sao tac gia dung Fuzzy TOPSIS?
  - Vi du lieu danh gia tu chuyen gia co tinh mo ho, kho luong hoa bang so chinh xac.
- Fuzzy TOPSIS tinh hang nhu the nao?
  - Tinh khoang cach cua moi loi ich den nghiem ly tuong duong va nghiem xau nhat, sau do tinh closeness coefficient. Gia tri cang lon thi xep hang cang cao.
- Tai sao usability dung dau?
  - Vi day la trai nghiem truc tiep ma nguoi dung cam nhan ngay khi tim kiem, chon va dat hang.
- Bai bao co han che gi lon nhat?
  - Mau nho, pham vi mot khu vuc, va ket qua chua duoc kiem chung bang du lieu nguoi dung thuc te.
